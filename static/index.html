About this data

This is publicly available data published by the City and County of Denver under Colorado open records law (C.R.S. 1-2-227). Only voters whose ballot has not yet been received are shown. The data is refreshed each morning during the election cycle.

What this data does NOT include
  • How anyone voted
  • Social Security numbers
  • Driver's license numbers
  • Email addresses or signatures
🛡️ Safety & Access Policy

Accessibility classifications are estimates based on address format only and do not reflect actual on-the-ground conditions.

All addresses
  • Always seek permission from the property owner or manager before entering private property.
  • Obey all posted signs. No-soliciting and no-trespassing signs must be respected.
  • If you do not feel safe, leave immediately.
Houses marked "Accessible"
  • Does not guarantee safe or legal access.
  • May be part of a gated community or have a locked gate, guard dog, or no-trespassing sign.
  • Verify conditions before approaching.
Apartments marked "Inaccessible"
  • Multi-unit buildings frequently require buzzer or key access.
  • Do not enter a secured building without permission from a resident or property manager.

These classifications are a convenience only and do not constitute legal advice. Contact your campaign coordinator with questions.

About this data

Publicly available under C.R.S. 1-2-227. Only voters whose ballot has not yet been received are shown. Refreshed each morning during the election cycle.

What this data does NOT include
  • How anyone voted
  • Social Security or driver's license numbers
  • Email addresses or signatures

Voter data is public record under C.R.S. 1-2-227 · Denver Elections Division

🛡️ Safety & Access

Accessibility classifications are estimates only based on address format.

  • Always seek permission before entering private property.
  • Obey posted no-soliciting and no-trespassing signs.
  • If you do not feel safe, leave immediately.
  • Apartments frequently require buzzer access. Do not enter without permission.
🗳️ 24-Hour Ballot Drop Boxes

Open 24/7 through June 30, 2026 at 7:00 PM. You can drop a Denver ballot in any Denver County drop box.

    📋 Terms of Use

    This tool is provided for civic engagement and ballot return assistance purposes only. All voter data displayed is publicly available pursuant to C.R.S. § 1-2-227, which requires Colorado county clerks to make voter registration and ballot status information available to the public.

    Use of this data is subject to the following restrictions under Colorado law:

    • Voter data may not be used for commercial purposes
    • Voter data may not be used to harass, intimidate, or harm any individual
    • Redistribution or resale of this data is prohibited

    By using this tool, you agree to use voter information solely to assist Denver residents in returning their mail ballots during the 2026 Primary Election. Any other use is prohibited.
